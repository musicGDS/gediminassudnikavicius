About

Welcome to my website. My name is Gediminas and I work with music and sound for film and media. I have 10 years of experience working in this field. Therefore, I learn new things every day working with new projects.

I have graduated as tonemester from the Royal Danish Academy of Music in 2016 wih focus on cross over projects between classical and rhythmic music. I have also graduated as sound designer from Lithuanian Academy of Music and Theatre focusing on Sound for Film and Music production.

My vision is to finish the product on time with the best quality. My goal that client stay happy with the result so my mix would be actively listened.
